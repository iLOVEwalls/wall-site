The wall lore:

One day, as they sat staring at the blank wall in front of them, the decision was weighing heavily on their mind. They had been pondering for hours, trying to come up with a username that felt unique and meaningful...

The wall in front of them, however, stood tall and silent, as walls often do. It had no intention of being interesting, but there was something about its sheer simplicity that felt... perfect. Uncomplicated. Strong, yet neutral...

As they gazed at it, the idea hit them like a quiet epiphany. A wall was something solid, something constant, something that didn't need to explain itself...

So, they typed it in—a single word, so easy and so subtle, yet somehow full of meaning. No frills, no extra fuss. Just "wall." And for the first time in a while, they felt at ease with their decision...

Sometimes, the best ideas come from the simplest things. And so, they became "wall"—a username that would stay as solid and unassuming as the wall that inspired it.
